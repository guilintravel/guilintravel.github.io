Water Activities in Guilin: Best Places for Rafting and More

  • Home|
  • Travel Blog|
  • Blog Details

Guilin, located in the southern part of China, is famous for its stunning natural landscapes, which include majestic limestone peaks, serene rivers, and beautiful karst formations. The city's rich geography makes it an ideal destination for water-based activities, and one of the most popular ways to explore the beauty of this region is through water sports. Guilin offers a range of exciting water activities, from leisurely boat rides to thrilling rafting experiences. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast or someone who enjoys a more laid-back experience, Guilin’s water activities promise something for everyone.

1. Li River Cruise

The Li River is perhaps the most iconic body of water in Guilin, attracting thousands of tourists every year. The river is renowned for its crystal-clear water, surrounded by dramatic karst peaks that create one of the most picturesque landscapes in China. The Li River Cruise offers a relaxing yet awe-inspiring way to experience this natural wonder.

Li River Cruise Highlights:

  • Scenic Views: The Li River cruise takes passengers on a journey through stunning landscapes, where you can admire the unique karst mountains, lush greenery, and traditional rural life along the riverbanks. The cruise typically starts in Guilin and ends in Yangshuo, offering about four hours of scenic travel.
  • Photography Opportunities: The Li River is one of the most photographed places in China. The misty mountains, reflection of the limestone peaks in the water, and traditional fishing boats provide excellent opportunities for photography, especially during the early morning or late afternoon when the lighting is perfect.
  • Relaxing Ride: While on the boat, passengers can sit back and enjoy the tranquility of the river, making it a great option for those looking to unwind while enjoying the views. Many cruise operators also offer additional services such as meals, tea, and cultural performances.

The Li River Cruise is a must-do activity for anyone visiting Guilin, offering an unparalleled opportunity to enjoy the city's natural beauty from the water.

2. Bamboo Rafting on the Yulong River

For those seeking a more immersive experience on the water, bamboo rafting on the Yulong River is a popular choice. The Yulong River, a tributary of the Li River, is a peaceful, slower-flowing river that winds through picturesque landscapes, offering an ideal setting for bamboo rafting. This activity has become one of the most popular ways to explore the countryside around Yangshuo.

Bamboo Rafting Experience:

  • Scenic Journey: Bamboo rafting on the Yulong River allows you to drift peacefully through scenic landscapes of lush greenery, traditional villages, and karst formations. The experience is incredibly serene, with the occasional sighting of local fishermen using traditional methods to catch fish.
  • Traditional Bamboo Rafts: The rafts used for this activity are made of bamboo poles lashed together, creating a stable platform that floats smoothly down the river. While the rafts are powered by a guide using a long pole, you can sit back and enjoy the calming flow of the water.
  • Cultural Insight: As you float along the river, you’ll have the chance to see traditional rural life up close. The villages and rice paddies lining the riverbanks provide a glimpse into the local culture and way of life. The river also passes by some historical landmarks, such as ancient bridges and temples, which add to the experience.

Bamboo rafting on the Yulong River is a perfect activity for nature lovers, photography enthusiasts, and anyone looking for a relaxing yet memorable water experience in Guilin.

3. White Water Rafting in Longsheng

For thrill-seekers, the White Water Rafting experience in Longsheng, located about two hours from Guilin, is a must. The area is known for its steep, fast-flowing rivers and dramatic mountainous landscapes. White water rafting in Longsheng offers a thrilling ride through rugged terrain, making it an exciting activity for those looking for an adrenaline rush.

White Water Rafting Experience:

  • Thrilling Rapids: Longsheng's rivers are known for their challenging rapids, providing an exhilarating rafting experience. The rapids are rated from Class II to Class IV, which means they offer a mix of challenging and moderately difficult sections. Adventurers can enjoy the thrill of navigating through these rapids, surrounded by stunning mountain views.
  • Professional Guides: For safety, rafting tours in Longsheng are typically led by professional guides who are skilled in navigating the white water. They provide essential instructions and ensure that everyone on the raft is equipped with safety gear such as life jackets and helmets.
  • Beautiful Scenery: While the focus is on the excitement of the rapids, the surrounding natural scenery is equally breathtaking. The lush forests, terraced rice fields, and towering mountains make for an unforgettable backdrop during the rafting adventure.

Rafting in Longsheng is an ideal activity for people looking for an adrenaline-pumping adventure in the midst of breathtaking natural beauty.

4. Hot Spring and River Tour in Guilin

For those looking to combine relaxation with water activities, a hot spring and river tour is an excellent choice. Guilin is home to several hot spring resorts, where visitors can enjoy the therapeutic benefits of mineral-rich waters while soaking in the surrounding scenery. Many of these hot springs are located along rivers, offering a unique experience where you can enjoy the warmth of the springs while being close to flowing rivers.

What to Expect:

  • Hot Spring Resorts: Guilin has several hot spring resorts that offer a range of services, from private hot spring pools to large public bathhouses. Some resorts are located near the Li River, allowing you to enjoy the serene views of the river while relaxing in the natural hot springs.
  • Therapeutic Benefits: The mineral-rich waters of Guilin's hot springs are known for their healing properties, which can help with relaxation, improve blood circulation, and relieve stress. The combination of the warm, soothing waters and the peaceful riverside surroundings creates a truly relaxing experience.
  • River Views: Many of the hot spring resorts are located in scenic areas, offering beautiful views of the rivers and mountains around Guilin. Some resorts even offer boat tours along the rivers, allowing you to enjoy the water and the landscape from a different perspective.

The hot spring and river tour is perfect for those looking for a more leisurely and rejuvenating water experience in Guilin.

5. Kayaking on the Li River

For those who want to take a more active role in their water experience, kayaking on the Li River offers an exciting way to explore the stunning landscapes of Guilin. Kayaking allows you to get closer to the water, offering a more personal and adventurous experience than the traditional Li River cruise.

Kayaking Experience:

  • Personalized Adventure: Kayaking on the Li River allows you to paddle along the river at your own pace, providing a more intimate experience with nature. You can explore smaller tributaries, islands, and hidden coves that larger boats cannot access, giving you a unique perspective of the surrounding landscape.
  • Stunning Views: The Li River is surrounded by breathtaking karst mountains, traditional villages, and lush forests, which you can enjoy while paddling along the river. The calm waters and dramatic landscapes make kayaking an unforgettable experience for outdoor enthusiasts.
  • Guided Tours: Many kayaking tours offer the option of hiring a local guide who can provide insights into the river’s history, ecology, and the surrounding area. Some tours also include a visit to local villages, allowing you to experience the culture of the region.

Kayaking on the Li River is ideal for those looking to combine adventure with scenic beauty, offering a great way to explore Guilin’s famous landscape while staying active.

6. River Tubing in Yangshuo

For a fun and relaxing water activity, river tubing in Yangshuo is a great option. The countryside around Yangshuo offers tranquil rivers with gentle currents, making it perfect for floating along the water on a rubber tube. This activity is easy-going, allowing you to relax and enjoy the stunning views as you float downstream.

What to Expect:

  • Leisurely Float: River tubing in Yangshuo is perfect for those who want to enjoy the beauty of the landscape at a slower pace. You can float lazily down the river while taking in the picturesque views of karst peaks, rice fields, and rural villages.
  • Family-Friendly: River tubing is a family-friendly activity, suitable for people of all ages. It’s a relaxing and enjoyable way to spend a day in the countryside, making it ideal for families with children.
  • Group Tubing: Many companies offer group tubing experiences, where you can join other tourists for a fun, social activity on the water. This is a great way to meet new people while enjoying the peacefulness of the river.

River tubing in Yangshuo is a laid-back way to experience the region’s natural beauty and is ideal for those seeking a relaxing water activity.

Guilin is a paradise for water enthusiasts, offering a wide variety of activities for all types of travelers. Whether you want to experience the excitement of white-water rafting, the tranquility of bamboo rafting, or simply enjoy the peaceful flow of the Li River, there’s something for everyone in this beautiful region. So, if you're planning a trip to Guilin, don’t miss the chance to explore its stunning rivers and indulge in some unforgettable water activities.
