How Is the Electric Scooter and Bicycle Rental Service in Guilin?

  1. Home
  2. Travel Blog

Guilin, a city famed for its picturesque karst landscapes and serene rivers, offers an ideal setting for exploring by electric scooter or bicycle. For tourists and locals alike, these modes of transportation provide a convenient, eco-friendly, and affordable way to navigate the city's natural and urban wonders. Whether you are visiting bustling city streets or tranquil rural trails, the rental services in Guilin cater to a variety of needs. Here's an in-depth guide to understanding the electric scooter and bicycle rental scene in Guilin.

1. The Growing Popularity of Electric Scooters and Bicycles in Guilin

In recent years, electric scooters (known locally as dian dong hua ban che) and bicycles (zi xing che) have gained popularity as efficient and eco-friendly transportation options. They are particularly appealing in Guilin due to the city's compact layout and scenic surroundings. Visitors often prefer these modes of transport to experience Guilin's charm at a leisurely pace without being confined to traditional vehicles.

Electric scooters are especially popular for their speed and ease of use, while bicycles are favored for their simplicity and suitability for more scenic routes, such as along the Li River or in the Yulong River Valley.

2. Where to Rent Electric Scooters and Bicycles in Guilin

Street-Side Rental Shops

  • Many rental shops are scattered around popular tourist areas like the city center, Xiangshan Park, and near the Li River.
  • These shops often display their scooters and bikes prominently, making them easy to spot.
  • Rental processes here are straightforward and typically involve showing a form of identification, such as a passport or local ID.

Hotels and Hostels

  • Many accommodations in Guilin provide scooter or bicycle rentals for their guests. These services are often competitively priced and convenient, especially for tourists who prefer to start their exploration from the doorstep.
  • Some high-end hotels may include bicycle rentals as part of their package, while smaller guesthouses may offer scooters for short-term use.

Mobile App-Based Rentals

  • App-based rentals, such as Mobike (known as Mo Bai) and Hello Bike, are widely available in Guilin.
  • These services allow users to locate and unlock bicycles or scooters using a smartphone app. They are particularly convenient for short trips and spontaneous exploration.
  • To use these apps, travelers typically need to download the app, register with a valid phone number, and link a payment method such as a credit card or local mobile payment system.

3. Cost of Renting Scooters and Bicycles

Electric Scooters

  • Prices vary depending on the rental duration and type of scooter. On average:
    • Hourly Rates: ¥20-30 ($3-5 USD) per hour.
    • Daily Rates: ¥80-150 ($12-20 USD) for a full day.
  • Some rental shops may offer discounts for multiple days or group rentals.

Bicycles

  • Bicycles are generally more affordable than scooters:
    • Hourly Rates: ¥10-20 ($1.50-3 USD) per hour.
    • Daily Rates: ¥50-80 ($7-12 USD) for a full day.
  • Tandem bicycles, suitable for couples or friends, may cost slightly more.

4. Popular Routes for Electric Scooters and Bicycles in Guilin

City Exploration

  • Xiangshan (Elephant Trunk Hill): Scooters and bikes are a convenient way to visit this iconic landmark without worrying about parking.
  • Two Rivers and Four Lakes Scenic Area: Cycling or scooting around the interconnected waterways is a relaxing way to explore Guilin’s heart.

Countryside Adventures

  • Yulong River Valley: This area is famous for its rural charm and bamboo rafting. The flat terrain makes it perfect for cycling.
  • Li River Bike Trail: Following the river’s edge, this route offers breathtaking views of karst mountains and idyllic countryside scenes. Electric scooters can be a good alternative for those wanting to cover longer distances.

5. Safety and Regulations

Helmet Use

  • Helmets are typically provided with scooter rentals, but their use is not always strictly enforced. It is highly recommended to wear one for safety.

Traffic Rules

  • While electric scooters and bicycles can use bike lanes, it’s essential to follow traffic rules. Scooters may need to stick to designated scooter lanes or share the road with cars in some areas.
  • Pedestrian zones often restrict scooter use, so be mindful of local regulations.

Age and Licensing Requirements

  • No special license is needed for electric scooters with lower speeds. However, some shops may restrict rentals to individuals over 16 years of age.
  • For high-speed scooters, shops may require additional documentation or a higher rental fee.

6. Advantages of Renting Electric Scooters and Bicycles in Guilin

Cost-Effective Travel

  • Both scooters and bikes are significantly cheaper than renting cars or relying on taxis for sightseeing.

Environmentally Friendly

  • With Guilin's emphasis on sustainability and preserving its natural beauty, these eco-friendly transportation modes align well with the city’s values.

Flexibility

  • Scooters and bikes allow travelers to explore at their own pace, taking detours to hidden spots or pausing to enjoy the scenery.

7. Challenges to Consider

Language Barriers

  • Many rental shop staff may not speak English fluently. Having basic knowledge of Chinese phrases or using a translation app can be helpful.

Weather Conditions

  • Guilin’s weather can be unpredictable, with sudden rain showers during certain seasons. It’s advisable to check the forecast and carry a raincoat or poncho when renting.

Limited Charging Stations for Scooters

  • Electric scooters need to be recharged after extended use. While some rental shops offer battery swaps, it’s essential to plan routes accordingly.

8. Tips for a Smooth Rental Experience

  • Inspect the Vehicle: Check brakes, tires, and battery life before renting.
  • Carry Identification: Always have your passport or ID ready, as many shops require it.
  • Understand Payment Options: Ensure clarity about the payment method and security deposit requirements.
  • Know the Local Routes: Researching popular routes or asking locals for recommendations can enhance your experience.

9. A Recommendation for First-Timers

If you're new to exploring Guilin by scooter or bike, start with a guided tour offered by some rental shops or travel agencies. These tours often include stops at key attractions, making them a stress-free way to experience the city and its surroundings.

Guilin’s scooter and bicycle rental services offer a unique way to immerse yourself in the city's enchanting landscapes. Whether you’re cycling along the Li River or scooting through city streets, these modes of transport provide the freedom to create unforgettable memories.
