Guilin Airport to Guilin University: Travel Guide

  • Home|
  • Travel Blog|
  • Blog Details

Guilin, a city renowned for its breathtaking karst landscapes and serene rivers, is also home to several prestigious educational institutions, including Guilin University. Whether you're a student, a visiting scholar, or a traveler exploring the city, getting from Guilin Liangjiang International Airport (KWL) to Guilin University can be a seamless experience with the right guidance. This travel guide covers everything you need to know—transportation options, scenic routes, nearby attractions, and insider tips to make your journey enjoyable.

Transportation Options from Guilin Airport to Guilin University

1. Airport Shuttle Bus

The most budget-friendly option is the airport shuttle bus. Line 1 operates from the airport to downtown Guilin, with stops near major transportation hubs. From there, you can transfer to a local bus or taxi to reach Guilin University.

  • Cost: Approximately ¥20-30
  • Travel Time: 40-50 minutes to downtown, plus transfer time
  • Pros: Affordable, frequent departures
  • Cons: Requires a transfer, may not be the fastest option

2. Taxi or Ride-Hailing Services

For a direct and hassle-free ride, taxis and ride-hailing apps like Didi are widely available.

  • Cost: ¥80-120, depending on traffic
  • Travel Time: 30-40 minutes
  • Pros: Door-to-door service, no transfers
  • Cons: Higher cost during peak hours

3. Private Transfers

If you prefer comfort and convenience, pre-booking a private transfer is ideal. Many hotels and travel agencies offer this service.

  • Cost: ¥150-250
  • Travel Time: 30 minutes
  • Pros: Personalized service, no waiting
  • Cons: More expensive than other options

4. Public Bus

For the adventurous traveler, public buses are an option, though they require multiple transfers.

  • Cost: ¥5-10
  • Travel Time: 60-90 minutes
  • Pros: Extremely cheap
  • Cons: Time-consuming, not recommended with heavy luggage

Scenic Stops Along the Way

If you have extra time, consider making a detour to these iconic spots:

Elephant Trunk Hill (Xiangbishan)

A short stop at this famous landmark—a natural rock formation resembling an elephant drinking from the Li River—is worth the visit.

Two Rivers and Four Lakes Scenic Area

This picturesque area connects the Li River and Peach Blossom River, offering stunning views and leisurely boat rides.

Zhengyang Pedestrian Street

A bustling street filled with local snacks, souvenirs, and vibrant nightlife—perfect for a quick exploration.

Nearby Attractions for Guilin University Visitors

Once you arrive at Guilin University, these nearby attractions are must-visits:

Reed Flute Cave (Ludi Yan)

A mesmerizing limestone cave illuminated by colorful lights, just a short drive from the university.

Seven Star Park (Qixing Gongyuan)

Guilin’s largest park, featuring hiking trails, caves, and the iconic Seven Star Peaks.

Guilin Night Market

Sample local delicacies like Guilin rice noodles (mifen) and beer fish at this lively market.

Insider Tips for a Smooth Journey

  • Avoid Rush Hour: Traffic can be heavy between 7:30-9:00 AM and 5:00-7:00 PM.
  • Carry Cash: While digital payments are common, some taxis or small vendors prefer cash.
  • Learn Basic Mandarin Phrases: Simple phrases like "Qù Guìlín Dàxué" (To Guilin University) can be helpful.
  • Check Weather: Guilin’s weather can be unpredictable—pack an umbrella during the rainy season.

With this guide, your trip from Guilin Airport to Guilin University will be as smooth as the city’s iconic river cruises. Enjoy your journey through one of China’s most enchanting destinations!
