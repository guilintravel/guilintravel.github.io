Guilin Travel Card: A Convenient Way to Explore the City

  • Home|
  • Travel Blog|
  • Blog Details

Guilin, with its breathtaking karst landscapes, serene rivers, and rich cultural heritage, is a must-visit destination for travelers. Navigating the city’s attractions, however, can sometimes be a hassle—especially when dealing with transportation, tickets, and discounts. That’s where the Guilin Travel Card comes in. This all-in-one pass is designed to make your trip smoother, cheaper, and more enjoyable.

Why the Guilin Travel Card is a Game-Changer

Traveling in a new city often means juggling multiple tickets, figuring out public transport, and hunting for discounts. The Guilin Travel Card eliminates these headaches by offering:

  • Unlimited access to public transportation (buses, ferries, and select shuttle services).
  • Discounted or free entry to major attractions like Elephant Trunk Hill, Reed Flute Cave, and the Li River cruises.
  • Exclusive deals at partner restaurants, shops, and cultural experiences.

Whether you’re a solo backpacker, a couple on a romantic getaway, or a family with kids, this card adapts to your travel style.

How the Card Works

The Guilin Travel Card operates on a simple tap-and-go system. Here’s what you need to know:

  1. Purchase Options:

    • 24-hour pass: Perfect for a quick stopover.
    • 3-day pass: Ideal for a weekend trip.
    • 7-day pass: Best for explorers who want to see everything at a relaxed pace.

  2. Activation:
    The card activates on first use and remains valid for the duration of your chosen plan. No need to worry about time zones or complicated setups.

  3. Where to Buy:

    • Online (via official tourism websites or apps).
    • At Guilin International Airport.
    • Major hotels and tourist information centers in the city.

Top Attractions Covered by the Card

1. Li River Cruise

No trip to Guilin is complete without a cruise down the Li River. The travel card offers discounted tickets for this iconic journey, where you’ll pass by limestone peaks, bamboo groves, and local fishing villages. Pro tip: Opt for the sunrise or sunset cruise for the most magical views.

2. Reed Flute Cave

Known as the “Palace of Natural Art,” this illuminated cave system is a surreal experience. The travel card grants you skip-the-line access, saving you precious vacation time.

3. Elephant Trunk Hill

This natural rock formation resembling an elephant drinking from the river is Guilin’s symbol. With the card, you’ll get free entry and a guided audio tour (available in multiple languages).

4. Longji Rice Terraces

While slightly outside the city, the terraces are worth the trip. The card includes a discounted round-trip shuttle service from Guilin, making it easier to visit this UNESCO-listed wonder.

Hidden Gems Accessible with the Card

Beyond the big-name attractions, the Guilin Travel Card unlocks lesser-known spots that most tourists miss:

· Seven Star Park

A sprawling green space with caves, temples, and a zoo. The card covers the entrance fee and a funicular ride to the park’s highest viewpoint.

· Fubo Hill

Offering panoramic views of the city, this hill is quieter than Elephant Trunk but equally stunning. The card includes free entry and a small souvenir from the onsite tea house.

· Night Markets

The card gives you discounts at Zhengyang Pedestrian Street and other night markets, where you can sample Guilin’s famous rice noodles (mifen) and local crafts.

Money-Saving Tips for Cardholders

To maximize your savings:

  • Plan your itinerary in advance: Group attractions by proximity to minimize transport costs.
  • Use partner restaurants: Show your card for 10–15% off meals at participating spots.
  • Check for seasonal promotions: During festivals like the Guilin International Tourism Festival, the card often includes extra perks.

Real Traveler Experiences

Here’s what recent visitors had to say:

  • “The card saved us over $50 per person. We rode buses everywhere and skipped ticket lines—worth every penny!” — Sarah, USA
  • “I loved the free tea tasting at Fubo Hill. Such a nice touch!” — Marco, Italy

Final Thoughts

The Guilin Travel Card isn’t just a ticket—it’s your key to a stress-free adventure. From misty mountains to bustling markets, this pass helps you experience the best of Guilin without the usual tourist frustrations. Pack your bags, grab your card, and get ready to fall in love with one of China’s most picturesque cities.
