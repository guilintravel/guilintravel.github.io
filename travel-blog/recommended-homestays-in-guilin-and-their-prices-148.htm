Recommended Homestays in Guilin and Their Prices

  • Home|
  • Travel Blog|
  • Blog Details

Guilin, with its breathtaking karst landscapes and serene rivers, is a dream destination for travelers. To make your stay truly memorable, choosing the right accommodation is essential. Homestays in Guilin offer a unique blend of local culture, personalized service, and scenic views, providing an immersive experience unlike standard hotels. Here, we explore some of the best homestay options in Guilin, their features, and what you can expect to pay.

Popular Homestay Areas in Guilin

Downtown Guilin

Staying in downtown Guilin provides easy access to popular attractions such as Elephant Trunk Hill, Seven Star Park, and the bustling Zhengyang Pedestrian Street. Homestays here are typically modern, stylish, and cater to travelers seeking convenience.

Yangshuo

Just a short drive from Guilin, Yangshuo is famed for its dramatic karst peaks and the Li River. Homestays in this area are often nestled amidst nature, offering tranquility and stunning views.

Longji Rice Terraces

For those looking to escape the city, the Longji Rice Terraces provide a serene retreat. Homestays here are often run by local ethnic families, offering an authentic cultural experience.

Recommended Homestays in Guilin

Zen Tea House

Located in downtown Guilin, Zen Tea House combines modern amenities with traditional Chinese aesthetics. Its rooms are minimalist yet comfortable, with large windows overlooking lush greenery.

  • Price: $50–$80 per night
  • Highlights: Tea-tasting experiences, proximity to city attractions, and a tranquil garden for relaxation.

Riverside Retreat

Situated near the Li River in Yangshuo, Riverside Retreat offers cozy rooms with panoramic views of the surrounding karst mountains.

  • Price: $40–$70 per night
  • Highlights: Outdoor pool, riverside dining, and easy access to cycling trails.

Secret Garden Boutique Hotel

Tucked away in the quiet village of Jiuxian in Yangshuo, this restored Qing Dynasty property offers a charming and intimate stay.

  • Price: $60–$100 per night
  • Highlights: Traditional architecture, organic meals, and a peaceful rural setting.

Longji One Art Hotel

Located in the heart of the Longji Rice Terraces, this boutique hotel is a favorite among those seeking a rustic yet comfortable experience.

  • Price: $70–$120 per night
  • Highlights: Stunning terrace views, locally inspired decor, and guided hiking tours.

Wada Hostel

Ideal for budget-conscious travelers, Wada Hostel is a lively and welcoming spot in downtown Guilin. It offers dormitory-style accommodations as well as private rooms.

  • Price: $10–$30 per night
  • Highlights: Social atmosphere, communal activities, and budget-friendly prices.

Mountain View Retreat

As its name suggests, this Yangshuo homestay boasts spectacular views of the karst mountains and is perfect for nature lovers.

  • Price: $50–$90 per night
  • Highlights: Outdoor terrace, family-friendly amenities, and proximity to Moon Hill.

Yunshe Boutique Hotel

Nestled by the Li River, this boutique homestay offers a luxurious escape with personalized service and modern comforts.

  • Price: $100–$150 per night
  • Highlights: Riverside location, exquisite decor, and tailored tour packages.

What to Expect from Guilin Homestays

Unique Decor and Atmosphere

Most homestays in Guilin are designed to reflect the region’s cultural heritage. Expect a mix of traditional Chinese elements such as wooden furniture, intricate carvings, and bamboo accents, combined with modern conveniences.

Personalized Service

Homestays often have fewer rooms than hotels, allowing hosts to provide individualized attention. Many owners are happy to share travel tips, arrange tours, or even cook meals for guests.

Scenic Surroundings

Whether you stay in downtown Guilin, Yangshuo, or the Longji Rice Terraces, homestays are strategically located to offer stunning views. From karst peaks to terraced fields, you’ll wake up to breathtaking landscapes.

Local Cuisine

Many homestays serve homemade meals featuring local specialties like Guilin rice noodles, beer fish, and sticky rice. Some even offer cooking classes where you can learn to prepare these dishes.

Tips for Choosing a Homestay

Budget

Decide your budget beforehand. Guilin has homestays ranging from budget-friendly to luxurious, so there’s something for everyone.

Location

Consider what type of experience you’re seeking. If you want to explore Guilin’s city attractions, choose a downtown homestay. For natural beauty, opt for Yangshuo or the Longji Rice Terraces.

Reviews

Read online reviews on platforms like TripAdvisor, Booking.com, or Airbnb. Pay attention to comments about cleanliness, service, and the overall experience.

Accessibility

If you’re traveling with children or have mobility issues, check whether the homestay is easily accessible. Some properties in the rice terraces require a bit of hiking to reach.

Amenities

Determine which amenities are important to you, such as Wi-Fi, air conditioning, or on-site dining. While most homestays in Guilin offer basic comforts, luxury options may include pools, spas, or guided tours.

Seasonal Considerations

The best time to visit Guilin is during spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November), when the weather is pleasant, and the scenery is at its most vibrant. Prices for homestays may be higher during these peak seasons, so booking in advance is recommended.

Booking a Homestay

  • Online Platforms: Websites like Agoda, Airbnb, and Ctrip offer a wide selection of homestays in Guilin.
  • Direct Contact: Some smaller homestays might not be listed online but can be contacted via phone or email.
  • Through Travel Agencies: Many agencies offer packages that include homestay accommodations.

Final Thoughts

Guilin’s homestays provide an unforgettable way to experience the region’s beauty and culture. With options ranging from rustic mountain retreats to chic boutique hotels, there’s a perfect fit for every traveler. Wherever you choose to stay, the warmth and hospitality of Guilin’s homestay hosts are sure to make your trip truly special.
