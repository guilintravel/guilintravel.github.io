Guilin Travel Card: The Best Deals for Foreign Tourists

  • Home|
  • Travel Blog|
  • Blog Details

Guilin, with its breathtaking karst landscapes, serene Li River, and vibrant cultural heritage, is a must-visit destination in China. For foreign tourists looking to explore this paradise without breaking the bank, the Guilin Travel Card is a game-changer. This all-in-one pass offers exclusive discounts, seamless access to top attractions, and insider perks that make your trip unforgettable.

Why the Guilin Travel Card is a Must-Have

Traveling as a foreigner in China can sometimes feel overwhelming—language barriers, ticketing hassles, and hidden fees can add unnecessary stress. The Guilin Travel Card eliminates these pain points by bundling the best of Guilin into one convenient package.

Unbeatable Savings on Top Attractions

The card grants discounted or even free entry to Guilin’s most iconic sites, including:
- Li River Cruise (a highlight of any Guilin trip)
- Reed Flute Cave (a mesmerizing underground wonder)
- Elephant Trunk Hill (Guilin’s symbol)
- Longji Rice Terraces (a photographer’s dream)

With the card, you could save up to 40% compared to buying individual tickets.

Hassle-Free Transportation

Getting around Guilin is a breeze with the card’s included perks:
- Unlimited public bus rides within the city
- Discounted taxi vouchers for longer trips
- Priority access to shuttle services for popular routes like Yangshuo

No more fumbling for change or struggling with ride-hailing apps!

Exclusive Perks for Foreign Tourists

The Guilin Travel Card isn’t just about savings—it’s about enhancing your experience.

VIP Treatment at Local Restaurants

Guilin’s food scene is legendary, and the card unlocks exclusive deals at top-rated spots:
- Yangshuo Beer Fish (a local delicacy) – Enjoy a 15% discount at partnered restaurants.
- Guilin Rice Noodles – Get a free side dish with your order.
- Night markets – Skip the line at popular stalls with your card.

Cultural Experiences Made Easy

For travelers eager to dive deeper into Guilin’s culture, the card offers:
- Free traditional cormorant fishing shows (a dying art form)
- Discounts on calligraphy or tea ceremony workshops
- Access to lesser-known villages with guided tours

How to Get Your Guilin Travel Card

Online Purchase (Recommended)

Buying the card before your trip is the smartest move:
1. Visit the official Guilin tourism website.
2. Select the card duration (3-day, 5-day, or 7-day pass).
3. Receive a digital QR code via email—no need for physical pickup!

On-Site Purchase

If you’re already in Guilin, head to:
- Guilin International Airport (kiosks near baggage claim)
- Major hotels (ask the concierge)
- Tourist information centers (downtown and in Yangshuo)

Pro tip: The online version often comes with extra discounts, so plan ahead!

Insider Tips for Maximizing Your Card

Plan Your Itinerary Around Card Benefits

Some attractions have time-sensitive deals. For example:
- Early bird discounts for sunrise at Longji Terraces
- Evening specials for night cruises on the Li River

Combine with Local Festivals

If your trip coincides with events like the Guilin International Tourism Festival, the card often includes free entry or VIP seating.

Share the Love (But Not the Card)

The card is non-transferable, but many partnered businesses offer "bring-a-friend" discounts—perfect for group travelers!

Real Traveler Stories

Sarah from Australia: "Saved Over $200!"

"I used the 7-day pass and visited almost every major site. The best part? The free boat ride to Yangshuo—it’s usually $50!"

Miguel from Spain: "No Language Struggles"

"The card’s included audio guides at attractions were a lifesaver. I learned so much without needing a translator."

Final Thoughts

The Guilin Travel Card isn’t just a ticket—it’s your golden key to unlocking the best of Guilin. From jaw-dropping landscapes to mouthwatering cuisine, this pass ensures you experience it all effortlessly. Whether you’re a solo adventurer or a family on vacation, don’t miss out on this traveler’s secret weapon.

Pack your bags, grab your card, and get ready for the Guilin adventure of a lifetime!
