Are There Family-Friendly Amusement Parks in Guilin?

  • Home|
  • Travel Blog|
  • Blog Details

Guilin, located in the Guangxi region of southern China, is renowned for its stunning natural landscapes, including the famous limestone karsts, serene rivers, and lush green hills. As a top travel destination, it’s no surprise that Guilin also offers a variety of attractions suitable for family visits, particularly for those traveling with children. While the city is mostly known for its breathtaking scenery and historical sites, it also boasts a range of family-friendly parks and amusement attractions that are perfect for a fun-filled day out with the kids.

Whether you’re looking for thrilling rides, interactive experiences, or outdoor adventures, Guilin has something to offer for every age group. The city’s family-friendly amusement parks and recreational areas provide an excellent opportunity for parents and children alike to enjoy quality time together. Here’s a look at some of the best places in Guilin where you can take your kids for a memorable family outing.

1. Guilin Merryland Resort

Guilin Merryland Resort is one of the largest and most popular theme parks in the area. Situated in the beautiful surroundings of Yangshuo, this resort is a fantastic choice for families looking for an all-in-one vacation spot. The park is divided into several themed sections, each offering unique attractions and activities that cater to different age groups.

For younger children, the park offers various gentle rides, including carousels, mini roller coasters, and Ferris wheels. Older kids will enjoy the more thrilling attractions, such as water slides, bumper cars, and other adrenaline-pumping rides. Additionally, there is a large water park area with wave pools, lazy rivers, and water slides that are perfect for cooling off on a hot day.

Guilin Merryland Resort also features beautiful landscapes and immersive entertainment options, including live performances, parades, and cultural shows that the whole family can enjoy. The resort’s proximity to the picturesque countryside adds to its charm, making it a great spot for a relaxing and fun-filled day.

2. Guilin Polar Ocean World

For families who are interested in marine life and aquatic animals, Guilin Polar Ocean World is an excellent choice. This expansive oceanarium is home to a variety of sea creatures from around the world, including polar bears, penguins, and dolphins. The park offers both indoor and outdoor exhibits, allowing visitors to explore marine life from different ecosystems.

Children will love the interactive experiences, such as the opportunity to watch live dolphin shows, pet sea animals, and learn about marine conservation. The park also features a large aquarium with colorful fish, sea turtles, and exotic species that will fascinate kids of all ages. Additionally, there are themed rides and play areas that are perfect for families with younger children.

Guilin Polar Ocean World is a great place to educate kids about the wonders of the ocean while providing a fun and exciting atmosphere. It’s an ideal choice for families looking for a more educational yet entertaining outing.

3. Guilin Amusement Park

Located in the heart of the city, Guilin Amusement Park is one of the oldest and most established amusement parks in the region. This park offers a classic theme park experience with a variety of rides, games, and attractions for all ages. The park is divided into several sections, each featuring different themes and types of entertainment.

For younger children, the park offers gentle rides like the carousel, mini train, and Ferris wheel. Older kids can enjoy more thrilling rides, such as the roller coasters and bumper cars. There are also arcade games, snack bars, and picnic areas, making it an ideal place for a fun and relaxing family outing.

Guilin Amusement Park is a great option for families who want to experience a traditional amusement park without venturing too far from the city center. With its wide range of attractions and family-friendly atmosphere, it’s a wonderful choice for a day of entertainment.

4. Guilin Xiongsen Bear and Tiger Mountain Village

For animal lovers, Guilin Xiongsen Bear and Tiger Mountain Village is a must-visit. This unique wildlife park is home to a variety of animals, including bears, tigers, and other exotic species. The park offers a range of interactive experiences where families can get up close to the animals and learn about their natural habitats.

The main attractions in the park are the bear and tiger shows, where visitors can watch trained animals perform various tricks. There are also opportunities to feed the animals and take pictures with them. The park’s layout is designed to resemble a natural habitat, providing both children and adults with a realistic and immersive experience.

In addition to the animal exhibits, the park features play areas, picnic spots, and walking trails that make it an excellent choice for a family day out in nature. The combination of animal encounters and outdoor exploration makes this park a fun and educational destination for children.

5. Lijiang River Scenic Area

For families who prefer outdoor adventures, the Lijiang River Scenic Area is a beautiful natural attraction near Guilin that offers plenty of family-friendly activities. The area is known for its picturesque landscapes, including dramatic limestone peaks, clear waters, and lush greenery. It’s a great place to enjoy a relaxing boat ride along the river while taking in the stunning views.

Many tour operators offer family-friendly boat cruises that take visitors along the Lijiang River, offering an opportunity to see the famous Karst mountains up close. The boat rides are a peaceful way to explore the area, and children will love the experience of being on the water.

In addition to the boat rides, the Lijiang River Scenic Area offers hiking, cycling, and photography opportunities, making it an excellent place for families who enjoy spending time outdoors. The area’s beauty and tranquility provide the perfect backdrop for a memorable family adventure.

6. Yangshuo Park

Located just outside of Guilin, Yangshuo Park is another great option for families looking for a day of outdoor fun. This park is famous for its beautiful natural landscapes, including the picturesque karst peaks and lush greenery that surround the area. Families can take leisurely walks along the park’s paths, enjoy a picnic by the river, or rent bikes to explore the nearby countryside.

One of the highlights of Yangshuo Park is the West Street, a vibrant pedestrian street lined with shops, cafes, and restaurants. The street is filled with local souvenirs, snacks, and cultural performances, making it a fun spot for families to explore together.

Yangshuo Park offers a variety of family-friendly activities, from bike rentals to outdoor performances, ensuring that both kids and adults will have a great time. The park’s relaxed atmosphere and beautiful setting make it an ideal location for a family outing.

7. Guilin Zoo

For families who love animals, the Guilin Zoo is a perfect destination. This large zoo is home to a wide variety of animals, including pandas, tigers, monkeys, and birds. The zoo also features a number of exhibits focused on endangered species and animal conservation.

Children will enjoy the opportunity to see their favorite animals up close, and they can learn about the different species through educational displays. The zoo also has several play areas and snack bars, making it a great spot to relax and enjoy a family day out. The Guilin Zoo offers an excellent combination of entertainment and education, making it an ideal destination for families with young children.

8. Guilin Water Amusement Park

For families visiting in the warmer months, the Guilin Water Amusement Park is a fantastic place to cool off and have fun. This water park offers a variety of water slides, wave pools, and lazy rivers, making it a great place for children to splash around and enjoy the water.

The park is designed with families in mind, with separate areas for younger children and more adventurous attractions for older kids. There are also plenty of shaded spots, rest areas, and snack bars, making it easy for families to relax between rides. Whether you’re looking to ride thrilling water slides or simply float down the lazy river, the Guilin Water Amusement Park offers hours of fun for the whole family.

Guilin offers a wide range of family-friendly activities and amusement parks that are perfect for families traveling with children. Whether you’re looking for thrilling rides, interactive animal encounters, or beautiful outdoor scenery, the city has something to offer for every family. From the exciting rides at Guilin Merryland Resort to the educational experiences at Guilin Polar Ocean World, there’s no shortage of fun and adventure in this beautiful city. So, pack your bags and get ready for an unforgettable family trip to Guilin!
